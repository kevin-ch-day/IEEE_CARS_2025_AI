\section{Conclusion and Future Work}

This paper presented a controlled framework for studying AI-assisted malware-research workflows from a defensive perspective. By focusing on observable artifacts and measurable defensive outcomes, the study avoids unsupported claims about AI authorship while still addressing an important cybersecurity concern.

Future work will expand the experimental dataset, improve dynamic-analysis instrumentation, and evaluate whether the observed differences remain consistent across additional tools, environments, and malware categories.
