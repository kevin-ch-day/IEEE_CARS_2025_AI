\section{Research Questions and Study Design}

This study is organized around the following research questions:

\textbf{RQ1:} What observable differences appear between baseline and AI-assisted malware-research workflows?

\textbf{RQ2:} Which static, dynamic, or detection-based measurements are most useful for comparing baseline and AI-assisted conditions?

\textbf{RQ3:} What defensive lessons can be drawn from the observed differences?

The study uses a controlled design in which baseline artifacts are compared against artifacts produced or modified through an AI-assisted workflow. The comparison does not attempt to prove real-world AI authorship. Instead, it evaluates measurable differences under known experimental conditions.
